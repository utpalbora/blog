About Me!

I am Utpal Bora, a doctoral student at IIT Hyderabad, India. My research area is Programming Languages and Compiler Optimizations. Currently I am working on improving vectorization opportunities in the LLVM compiler infrastructure. My research interest also includes polyhedral compilation techniques.

My hometown is Jorhat. A place 280km to East of Guwahati. I have been living out of my hometown for past 11 years and I miss every now and then the greenary and frequent showers. Hoping to go back to Assam pretty soon.
